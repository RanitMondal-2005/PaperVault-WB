Empowering Academic Excellence

PaperVault WB is a specialized digital repository designed to bridge the gap between West Bengal's academic history and its digital future.

Our Mission

To provide students and educators with a seamless, verified, and organized platform for accessing previous years' examination papers. We believe that accessibility to quality resources shouldn't be a hurdle in a student's journey toward success.

Our Vision

To become the standard decentralized archive for West Bengal institutions, integrating cutting-edge analysis to help students identify trends and better prepare for their future careers.

Verified Content

All papers are vetted and uploaded exclusively by registered faculty members.

Open Access

A decentralized repository ensuring 24/7 availability for students across the state.

Smart Analysis

Future-ready infrastructure designed to support intelligent paper analysis and trends.

Join the Academic Revolution

Whether you are a student looking for resources or a faculty member wanting to contribute, PaperVault WB is your home.
